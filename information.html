Who is eligible?

  • All grandchildren and great-grandchildren of Art and Eileen Ranly under the age of 24 are eligible.

How much is available per individual?

  • Up to $5,000 per calendar year for tuition assistance
  • Maximum of 4 years of benefit
  • Maximum of $20,000 per individual
  • Payments are made after getting a copy of tuition bill and any scholarships.
    • If tuition bill is $1500, $1500 will be paid.
    • If tuition bill is $6000, $5000 will be paid.
    • If tuition is $6000, and there is a $2500 scholarship, $3500 will be paid.

What are the guidelines?

  • Must carry a 2.0 GPA to continue to get assistance.
  • If pass fail grading, must be passing.

When will the fund begin providing assistance?

  • January 1, 2024


* Eligibility, amounts, and guidelines subject to change.
